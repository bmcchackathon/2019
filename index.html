Mission

Start Coding Here. Go Coding Anywhere.

BMCC Hackathon is one day hackathon taking place on April 6th, hosted at Borough of Manhattan Community College. Come build your dream project, learn from our talks and mentors, and win prizes! BMCC Hackathon is free, and made possible thanks to our wonderful supporters and BMCC clubs.

The purpose of this hackathon is to inspire BMCC students to work in a collaborative environment to solve real world problems by creating needed projects while helping BMCC students familiarize with current technology.

What is a Hackathon?

An event for students to collaborate and learn. Students come together and solve problems while attend workshops


What should I bring ?
